# intro-data-science
## Final Project Ideas

### Idea 1: Gender Inequality in Engineering & Tech
This project explores gender inequality in STEM fields by analyzing data related to employment, education, and participation in technology and engineering.
* How does female representation vary across different STEM fields and industries?
* Are there patterns or clusters of regions with similar levels of gender inequality in tech?

### Idea 2: Urban Infrastructure & Quality of Life
This project investigates how access to infrastructure (such as parks, transportation, or public services) affects quality of life in cities.
* Do cities with better infrastructure show higher indicators of livability?
* Can cities be grouped into clusters based on infrastructure and resource distribution?

### Idea 3: Technology Access & Social Inequality
This project explores how access to technology differs across regions and how it impacts social and economic outcomes.
* Are there clusters of regions with similar levels of technology access?
* How does access to technology relate to income, education, or opportunity?
